Comprando casa en Austin? Lo hacemos juntos.

Every step - the tours, the offer, the contract, the lender calls, the closing table - runs in the language you think in.

  • Contracts explained line by line in Spanish or English, so you sign knowing exactly what you agreed to.

04 — Meet Javier

From Jalisco to Chicago to home in Austin.

Javier Melendez was born in Jalisco, Mexico, grew up between Chicago and Austin, and has spent more than twelve years making Central Texas home. He studied advertising at Texas State University, which is a fancy way of saying he knows how to make a home look its best and get it in front of the right people.

But the reason clients stay with him is simpler. He treats buying or selling a home like what it is - one of the biggest decisions of your life - and he brings knowledge, straight talk, and dedication to every step. In English or in Spanish, you always know where things stand.
